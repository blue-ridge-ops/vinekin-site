Features

Everything you need to grow in Scripture

Not another reading plan you'll abandon. VineKin uses proven learning science to help God's Word take root.

5-Minute Lessons

Bite-sized interactive lessons that fit between school drop-off and your first meeting. Learn through doing, not just reading.

Verse Mastery

Memorize Scripture through word ordering, fill-in-the-blank, reference identification, and spaced repetition. Actually remember what you read.

Personalized Devotionals

365 days of devotionals that connect Scripture to your real life — parenting, work, marriage, and the beautiful chaos in between.

Soul Friends & Small Groups

Inspired by the Celtic "anam cara" tradition. Walk with an accountability partner or grow in a small group of 3–10 with shared devotionals and discussion prompts.

Life-Stage Prayers

Guided prayers for pivotal moments: a child's first day of school, job loss, new baby, marital struggles. Tailored to your tradition and season of life.

Grace-First Design

Forgiving streaks, flexible weekly goals, and zero guilt trips. We earn "Seeds" not points, and your journey is your own. Life happens — your Bible app gets that.

Examination of Conscience

Guided reflection through multiple frameworks — Ten Commandments, Beatitudes, Fruit of the Spirit — with personalized Scripture surfaced for every struggle.

Dinner Table Starters

100+ age-appropriate conversation starters to help you bring Scripture into everyday family moments — bedtime, car rides, and dinner. You lead the conversation; we give you the words.

Adaptive Learning

Placement testing meets you where you are. The app adjusts difficulty based on your knowledge so beginners build confidence and veterans stay challenged.

How It Works

Start growing in three simple steps

1

Tell us about your life

Pick your tradition, share your life stage, and let us know what matters to you. We personalize everything — devotionals, prayer styles, and the Bible translation you see first.

2

Learn for five minutes a day

Interactive lessons teach through practice, not lectures. Arrange verses, fill in blanks, answer reflection questions — and actually retain what you learn through spaced repetition.

3

Watch it take root

Over weeks and months, see Scripture weave into your conversations, parenting, and daily decisions. Invite a Soul Friend or start a small group to grow alongside you.
